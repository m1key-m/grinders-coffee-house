Where it all began

Grinders Coffee House opened its doors for the first time in 2018 in Bristol! Opened by two good friends who had spent 5 years working as managers in a well-known national coffee chain that we will not mention for legal reasons! Mikey & Laura have more than 20 years experience between them, working their way up from pot wash to Store Manager, with experience in all aspects of the coffee making process. Grinders was born out of both necessity and frustration, over the years we realised that it was no longer about the coffee, but about profit. Now, we don't think profit is a bad thing, but when the profit is used to line the pockets of people you've never met and not used to invest in the team, to us that's a huge problem, and we'd like to change that.

Here at Grinders, we've brought the focus back to the coffee. Our menu is simple and very well priced (check out the reviews if you don't believe us) and our baristas are treated so well, they actually enjoy coming to work! We never imagined that we'd be this successful so early in, but in 5 years we've gone from one small venue in the centre of Bristol, to relocating to a place twice the size and opening two other stores in London & Cardiff. We don't expect you just to take our word for it, come in and find out for yourself! Check out where we are below!
